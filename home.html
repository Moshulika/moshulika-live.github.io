Carina Rosanna Tăutu

Short biography

Carina Rosanna Tăutu is a professional mediator, conflict coach, consultant, filmmaker and the host/producer of Mediator’s Talk webcast for the Beverly Hills Bar Association.

Her training is a blend of scientific discernment, deep discipline earned from
competitive sports, spiritual practice, and artistic imagination.

Born in Bucharest, Romania, she also studied Neuroscience, and was a
member of the Romanian national figure skating team for 8 years.
Carina worked on four continents and lived in New York and Paris
before moving to Los Angeles. She studied contemplative arts
for 20 years. Ashtanga Yoga and Argentine Tango are her anchors,
and so is her rescued black cat, Cloud.

Carina earned an MSc in Neuroscience from University of Bucharest, an MFA in film from Columbia University and Master in Dispute Resolution from Pepperdine Law/Straus Institute of Dispute Resolution.
